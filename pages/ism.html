I Slay Mobs

The Meme Comes Alive in Minecraft

I slay mobs is a mod that references the Minecraft parody I made back in 2015 and remastered in 2019 To get the record you just have to simply slay mobs. its roughly a 1/50 drop. you must have completed the monster slayer advancement first. In order to get the remastered version. released in 2019 simply combine the record with a wooden sword
