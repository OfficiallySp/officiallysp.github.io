Sonic Unite

Sonic Fangame for SAGE 22

Sonic Unite is a 2D Sonic Fangame built in Unity (Dont let that put you off), It aims to be a simple fangame that adds new concepts while keeping core Sonic gameplay elements from the original trilogy. as such, the aim is to not reinvent the wheel. Game physics are accurate and should just feel like a continuation of any other sonic game with a few QOL enchancements and exciting new level ideas.
