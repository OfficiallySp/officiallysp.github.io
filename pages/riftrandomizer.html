RiftRandomizer

League Customs Turned up to 11

RiftRandomizer is a tool used to randomly shuffle players and create unique gamemodes in both League Of Legends And VALORANT. A Custom version is used by the streamer BigCheeseGames with additional functionality, however a free version is available for the general public to use.
