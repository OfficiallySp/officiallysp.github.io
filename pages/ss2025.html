Tournament Rules & Prizes

Rules:

  1. Teams must consist of 5 main players and up to 2 optional substitutes. All roster changes must be submitted to tournament organizers at least 7 days before the tournament begins.
  2. Substitutes may only play if a main roster player is unavailable, not for strategic advantage. Teams must notify organizers before making substitutions.
  3. Players may only compete for one team throughout the entire tournament.
  4. All participants must provide their summoner name & Tag and a valid op.gg/u.gg profile for verification.
  5. Each team is limited to a maximum of two players ranked Master or higher (including those who were Master+ within the past 12 months).
  6. Tournament matches may be streamed by participants with a mandatory minimum 90 second delay to prevent stream sniping. (Stream Delay Guide)
  7. All matches require full 5-player teams. Teams with fewer than 5 players at match time will forfeit regardless of match outcome.
  8. Game pauses are permitted for technical issues only, with a maximum total pause time of 30 minutes per match. Both teams must agree to resume play.
  9. The following violations will result in immediate disqualification:
    • Use of third-party software, scripts, or any form of cheating
    • Excessive toxicity, hate speech, or harassment beyond reasonable competitive banter
    • Account sharing or playing under another player's credentials
    • Intentionally losing or throwing matches
    • Team names or logos that violate Riot Games' Terms of Service
    • Harassment or inappropriate behavior toward other participants
  10. All disputes between teams must be directed to tournament officials and not resolved in-game or in public channels.
  11. Tournament officials have final decision-making authority on all rule interpretations. Ignorance of rules is not an acceptable defense.
  12. All players must be 18 years or older.
